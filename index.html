About Me

In 2017 I began my working journey into the IT field by starting a job in Technical Support for a software company. I quickly learned how our software worked in regards to its day to day use but learned it relied on the SQL language. This is where my journey into Software Development began.

While learning SQL I found enjoyment and challenge in figuring out how different statements worked as well as finding a way to craft more complex statements to fulfill my needs. This started my process of what else could I learn. With the help of several members of the development team as well as friends I had met through my gaming life I dived deeper into the world of Development.

Now I am in school to earn my Computer Science BA with the goals of transitioning out of Support and moveing into Development. Each day is a new step on this journey and a new opportunity to learn.
